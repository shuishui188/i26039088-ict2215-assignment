Explore the City

The Paris of the East

Shanghai is not just a city; it is a timeline of the future and a museum of the past. From the colonial architecture of the Bund to the futuristic spires of Lujiazui, every corner tells a story of ambition and elegance.

The Heritage

Walk along the Waitan. Witness 52 buildings of varying architectural styles, representing a century of history.

The Future

Cross the river to Pudong. Stand beneath the Shanghai Tower and look up at the clouds.

City Highlights

Curated experiences for the modern traveler

Shikumen Living

Explore Xintiandi and Tianzifang. These traditional lane houses have been transformed into trendy boutiques and cafes, blending old Shanghai with new luxury.

Culinary Art

From the sweet and savory Xiaolongbao (soup dumplings) to the world-class cocktail bars hidden in the French Concession.

Art & Culture

Visit the Power Station of Art or the West Bund. Shanghai is rapidly becoming the art capital of Asia.
